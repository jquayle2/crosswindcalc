Rotary Knob Controls

Four tactile knobs for runway, wind direction, speed, and gusts. Touch to jump, drag to fine-tune. Designed for one-handed cockpit use.

RWY

Instant Crosswind

See your crosswind and headwind components update in real time as you adjust any parameter. Color-coded severity at a glance.

G

Gust Factor Aware

Separate gust speed input with Vref adjustment advisories. Know exactly how much extra speed to carry for gusty conditions.

✓

Pilot Advisories

Automatic recommendations for flap reduction when crosswinds exceed 10 knots. Directional indicators show which side the wind hits.

How It Works

Spin. Read. Fly.

01

Dial in your runway

Spin the amber RWY knob to your active runway heading. The dial snaps to standard runway numbers 01–36.

02

Set the wind

Use the blue WIND and SPEED knobs to match the reported winds from ATIS, AWOS, or your METAR.

03

Add gusts if applicable

Dial up the orange GUST knob. The app automatically calculates the additional Vref speed you should carry.

04

Read your crosswind

The top display shows your crosswind component, headwind, wind side, and any pilot advisories — all in real time.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Your Data

CrosswindCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network calls, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

What We Store

The only data saved is your last-used dial settings (runway, wind direction, speed, and gust), stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

CrosswindCalc does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising frameworks. There is zero data sharing with any third party.
